Café Samaya

A beachfront restaurant and cocktail bar.

Welcome to Everyone

Café Samaya is our beachfront restaurant and cocktail bar open to both hotel guests and external visitors. Enjoy a relaxed but elegant social atmosphere by the ocean. We operate on a walk-in basis—no reservations required.

Morning

Start your day slowly with the sound of the waves. Join us for locally sourced coffee, fresh juices, and a nourishing breakfast to ground your body and spirit.

8:00am - 11:30am

Day

As the sun rises high, cool down with our fresh tropical cuisine. Light, vibrant dishes made from island ingredients, perfect for a beachside lunch.

12:00pm - 4:00pm

Evening

Watch the sunset fade into the night. Share dinner, curated cocktails, and meaningful conversations in a warm, connected atmosphere.
