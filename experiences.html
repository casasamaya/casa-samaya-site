Experiences

Discover Sri Lanka's Southern Coast

Explore Weligama & Beyond

From the waves of the Indian Ocean to ancient forts and wild savannas, our team is here to help you experience the true rhythm of Sri Lanka.

Surf Lessons

Weligama is world-renowned for its perfect beginner and intermediate waves. We offer personalized surf lessons directly in front of Casa Samaya or at secret spots nearby.

Whale Watching in Mirissa

Set sail at sunrise to witness the majestic blue whales and playful dolphins in their natural habitat, just a short boat ride from Mirissa harbor.

Safari in Udawalawe

Embark on an unforgettable day trip into the wild. Spot wild elephants, leopards, and exotic birds in one of Sri Lanka's most beautiful national parks.

Day Trip to Galle

Wander through the cobblestone streets of the historic Galle Fort. Discover colonial architecture, boutique shops, and charming cafes.

Tea Plantation Visits

Take a scenic journey inland to explore the rolling green hills of a traditional Ceylon tea estate. Experience the art of tea plucking and tasting.

Let Us Organize Your Journey

Our concierge team is available to arrange transportation, guides, and customized itineraries for any of these experiences.
