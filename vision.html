Our Vision

"To create a space where time slows down"

Where Body, Heart & Spirit Align

There are places that invite you to slow down.
And there are places that invite you to return to yourself.

Casa Samaya was born from this intention: to create a coastal sanctuary where simplicity meets soul. Nestled between the ocean and the palm trees of Weligama, our house is not a resort. It is a rhythm. A quiet flow. A space where every detail is designed to breathe with you.

The Meaning of SAMAYA

The name SAMAYA comes from Sanskrit. It means sacred accord: a vow to live in alignment with oneself and the world. It unfolds in three syllables, three breaths, three dimensions of harmony:

• SA — Grounding

Rest, simplicity, and connection with the body. The art of stillness, the calm of our rooms, the serenity of space.

• MA — Presence

Human connection, shared moments, and social energy. The pleasure of a meal cooked with care, the warmth of the café.

• YA — Awareness

Inner balance, growth, and mindful practices. Surf, yoga, meditation, and ocean rituals to reconnect you to your natural rhythm.

Together, these three currents form the essence of Casa Samaya: a living house where comfort, consciousness, and community meet.

We believe in elegance without ostentation, in beauty that breathes, in experiences that speak softly but stay deeply. Here, time expands, and life finds its right tempo: between sunrise surf and sunset meditation.

Welcome home, to the art of conscious living.
